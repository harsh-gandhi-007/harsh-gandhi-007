About US

I developed a strong interest in the use of numerical computation for answering difficult questions in Thermal engineering and Fluid dynamics, and in particular, Motion of non-Newtonian fluid over accelerating geometries is abundantly observed in many industrial and biological processes, such as paper pulp industries, food industries, paint industries, dairy industries, etc. This study is primarily focused on power-law type rheology models. I am interested in determining the effect of non-Newtonian models on fluid dynamics.
